# VGrade

VGrade is a simple and modern GPA & CGPA calculator designed for VIT students. It helps you quickly calculate your semester GPA and updated CGPA through a clean, user-friendly interface.

## How to Use

### GPA Calculation

1. Enter credits and grade for each subject.
2. Add more subjects as needed.
3. Click **Calculate GPA** to get your result.
4. Download your result for future reference.

### CGPA Calculation

1. Enter your previous CGPA.
2. Enter total credits completed so far.
3. Enter your current semester GPA.
4. Enter credits taken this semester.
5. Click **Calculate CGPA** to get your result.

## GPA Calculation

GPA is calculated using the credits and grades entered for all subjects in the current semester.

## CGPA Calculation

CGPA is calculated using:

* Previous CGPA
* Previous total completed credits
* Current semester GPA
* Current semester credits

The calculation follows VIT's credit-weighted grading methodology to provide an accurate cumulative GPA.
